## 发布 (fābù)

 发布是指正式发布、发布或公布信息、公告或内容，是新闻稿、官方通知以及数据或数字内容发布的标准术语。 

## 含义

 1. **[动词]** 释放； 发布（官方声明或公告）
 2. **[动词]** 发布； 发布（信息、内容或数据）

 ## 例句

 **气象局发布了暴雨橙色预警信号。** Qìxiàngjú fābùle bàoyǔ chéngsè yùjǐng xìnhào. The meteorological bureau issued an orange-level heavy rain warning.

 **公司将于今晚发布最新财务报告。** Gōngsī jiāng yú jīn wǎn fābù zuìxīn cáiwù bàogào. The company will release its latest financial report tonight.

 **该政府部门定期发布统计数据供公众查阅。** Gāi zhèngfǔ bùmén dìngqī fābù tǒngjì shùjù gōng gōngzhòng cháyuè。 The government department regularly releases statistical data for public reference.

 **他在社交媒体上发布了一条声明，回应质疑。** Tā zài shèjiāo méitǐ shàng fābùle yī tiáo shēngmíng, huíyìng wàijiè zhiyí。 He posted a statement on social media to respond to external doubts.

 **苹果公司宣布将在秋季发布新款操作系统。**苹果公司xuānbù jiāng zài qiūjì fābù xīn kuǎn cāozuò xìtǒng。 Apple announced it will release a new operating system in the fall.

 **研究机构发布的报告显示，该地区的空气质量有所改善。** 延九地区空气质量有所改善。** 延九地区空气质量有所改善。 A report released by the research institution shows that air quality in the region has improved.

 ## 搭配

 | 搭配| 意义|
 |-------------|---------|
 | 发布公告 (fābù gōnggào) | issue an announcement |
 | 发布声明 (fābù shēngmíng) | release a statement |
 | 发布报告 (fābù bàogào) | release a report |
 | 新闻发布会 (xīnwén fābù huì) | press conference |
 | 发布预警 (fābù yùjǐng) | issue a warning |
 | 官方发布 (guānfāng fābù) | official release |

 ## 使用说明

 发布是正式发布或发布信息的标准动词，特别与政府公告、新闻稿、天气警报和数据发布相关。 In digital contexts, it also means to post or publish content online.

 The compound 新闻发布会 (press conference) is one of the most common collocations. Compared with 推出 (tuīchū, to launch a product), 发布 is more about the release of information and announcements. Compared with 出版 (chūbǎn, to publish a book), 发布 refers to non-book media and official communications.

 ## 记忆钩子

 FA (发, to send out/release) + BU (布, to spread/announce) = to send out and spread information widely.
